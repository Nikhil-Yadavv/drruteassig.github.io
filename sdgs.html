BWWCS's commitment to sustainability is making a positive impact on
several United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

By constructing wastewater treatment plants, rejuvenating water bodies, reclaiming land from dumpsites, and converting plastic waste to RDF for energy, the company is promoting clean water and sanitation, sustainable cities and communities, responsible consumption and production, climate action, marine and coastal biodiversity, and land restoration. Additionally, these efforts align with SDGs related to good health and well-being, affordable and clean energy, and industry, innovation, and infrastructure.

SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being - Your construction of wastewater treatment plants and rejuvenation of water bodies contribute to SDG 3 by improving access to clean water, which is essential for good health and well-being. By preventing the discharge of untreated wastewater into water bodies, you are also helping to reduce waterborne diseases, which can have a significant impact on public health.

SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation - Your company's construction of wastewater treatment plants and rejuvenation of water bodies contribute to SDG 6 by improving access to clean and safe water.

SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy - Your companies conversion of plastic waste to RDF and burning it for energy contributes to SDG 7 by promoting the use of affordable and clean energy. By generating energy from waste, you are helping to reduce reliance on fossil fuels and promote sustainable energy practices.

SDG 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure - Your companies activities contribute to SDG 9 by promoting sustainable infrastructure development and innovation. By reclaiming land from dumpsites and using waste materials to create new products, you are promoting sustainable use of resources and reducing waste. Additionally, your construction of wastewater treatment plants and rejuvenation of water bodies involve the use of innovative technologies and practices that help to promote sustainable infrastructure development.

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities - Your company's activities help in creating sustainable communities by providing clean water and sanitation, reclaiming land from dumpsites, and creating energy from waste.

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production - Your company's reuse of waste, converting plastic to RDF and burning it for energy, and making plastic products for reuse by compressing the waste contribute to SDG 12 by promoting responsible consumption and production.

SDG 13: Climate Action - Your company's activities contribute to SDG 13 by reducing carbon emissions through the conversion of plastic to RDF and burning it for energy instead of landfilling it.

SDG 14: Life Below Water - Your company's rejuvenation of water bodies and lakes up to 5 acres contribute to SDG 14 by promoting marine and coastal biodiversity.

SDG 15: Life on Land - Your company's activities contribute to SDG 15 by reclaiming land from dumpsites, promoting land restoration, and reducing land pollution.
